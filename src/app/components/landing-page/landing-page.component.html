About us

We provide a full delivery cycle of goods, documents and all this stuff. DIBER offers a door to door collection and delivery service through more than 100 local couriers, offering simple, convenient and affordable parcel delivery. When you ship with DIBER – you’re shipping with specialists in international shipping and courier delivery services! With our wide range of express parcel and package services, along with shipping and tracking solutions to fit your needs – learn how DIBER can deliver! Start shipping today!

Courier

DIBER couriers are recruited from within the areas that they live, which means that their local knowledge is second to none. For example if your customer/recipient isn't at home but works around the corner, our couriers will usually be only too happy to make a detour to ensure the customer receives their parcel as soon as possible.

Customer

Whether you’re shopping online or in store, you can afford to have someone else do the heavy lifting and deliver your purchases to your home or office. No matter the size or number of items in your order, you can now get it all delivered starting at $1! Because sometimes your ideas are bigger than your car.

Shop

We offer a few options for connection to our infrastructure. Your shop can have an automatic delivery of goods ordered by your customers in the store. Our couriers will be happy to work with your store and their will do best to deliver a goods on time and with pleasure to your customers! Our service is ready to support your business!
